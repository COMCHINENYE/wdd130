History

Whitewater rafting isn't just a modern thrill, it's a journey that began over 200 years ago. Originally used for transporting goods down wild rivers, rafting evolved into an adrenaline-packed adventure sport in the early 1900s. By the 1970s, guided rafting trips became popular worldwide, offering excitement, teamwork, and unforgettable scenery. Today, it's one of the most loved outdoor experiences, bringing people closer to nature, challenge, and pure fun all while floating through the heart of breathtaking landscapes.
